Market Context

Why This Colour Is Extraordinary

"Natural platinum white is the unicorn of the human hair world. Wig makers and collectors actively search for years without finding a donor at this length, this quality, and this colour combined."

Most white or silver-toned wigs are produced by bleaching dark or blonde hair — a process that compromises cuticle integrity, reduces tensile strength, and shortens the lifespan of the finished piece. Achieving this colour naturally is not repeatable on demand. It is the result of a genetic process that cannot be manufactured.

At 19 inches, this hair is long enough to produce a full, flowing wig or premium extensions without blending. The combination of length, condition, and colour makes this among the rarest raw hair available on the open market anywhere in the world.

At the time of listing, fewer than six white/grey hair listings of any length existed globally on the world's largest hair marketplace. None matched this combination of length and condition.
